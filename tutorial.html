So you want to improve your French or watch a specific French Canadian show with your partner?

This extension is created to be used in conjunction with the trifecta of French Canadian streaming websites: Noovo.ca, Tou.tv, and TeleQuebec, with the goal of French language learning in mind.

Having trouble accessing the video contents because you are outside of Canada? Well, I suggest you move to Canada, it's a great country. Or alternatively, you can get a VPN and change your IP address to be somewhere in Canada.

Traditou takes advantage of Google's auto-translation function to display English subtitles in real-time. This is the only reason that this extension can stay free. But also because of this, the translations can be a little laggy compared to the original French subtitles.

How to display both French and English subtitles:

  1. Right click to select "Translate to English" or use Chrome's built-in Google translate icon on the top right to translate the page to English
  2. Make sure Traditou is enabled.
